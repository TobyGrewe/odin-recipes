Root Beer Float Recipe

Directions: Place 1 scoop of ice cream into each of two tall glasses. Pour root beer carefully over the ice cream. Add another scoop and repeat. If possible, repeat again. Top each with whipped cream and cherries.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pint vanilla ice cream
  • 12 fluid ounce bottle of root beer
  • 1/2 cup whipped cream
  • 4 maraschino cherries

Steps

  1. Place 1 scoop of ice cream into each tall glass.
  2. Pour root beer carefully over the ice cream.
  3. Add another scoop and repeat.
  4. If possible, repeat again.
  5. Top each with whipped cream and cherries.
